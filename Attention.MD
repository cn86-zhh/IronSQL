# zh_CN

- 我们不建议使用来自MSVC的编译器与CMakeLists.txt
- 我们的标准是基于GNU/MinGW的G++编译器,而不是MSVC的标准.
- 所以, 当使用MSVC的编译器时, 可能会遇到一些问题, 甚至会导致编译失败.

# en_US

- We do not recommend using the compiler from MSVC with CMakeLists.txt
- Our standard is based on the GNU/MinGW G++ compiler, not the MSVC standard.
- So, when using the MSVC compiler, you may encounter some problems, or even cause compilation to fail.
